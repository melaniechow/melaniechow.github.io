HICE

HICE on my Wrist
June 2019

Hice is a body temperature regulation device. It serves as both a portable hand-warmer and ice-pack by detecting the temperature of your skin. When you're cold, HICE warms you up, and when you're hot, HICE cools you down.

See the Demo

SecURL

HCI Usable Security
May 2019

SecURL is a browser web-extension that analyzes the security of a website's certificates. Given a URL, it provides a data-driven explanation of what a given certificate is telling the user in an easy-to-read way.

Destress Cyborg

Uncommon Hacks 2019
Most Uncommon Hack (1st Place) & Best IoT Hack

Destress Cyborg (proof-of-concept) is a wearable tech that analyzes brain waves to blur out/replace visual objects that induce stress.

It uses an EEG headband and a camera to recognize visual triggers, and is made portable using a Qualcomm DragonBoard 401c

Compliance Carnivores

Content Carnivores
Fall 2018 - Winter 2019

Compliance Carnivores is a project exploring the compliance of online financial content with SEC/FINRA regulations. I worked with another intern to compile research on "bad actor" companies and case laws, and I solely built a platform to detect future compliance violations.

SafeRoute

ByteHacks 2018

SafeRoute is a web application that determines the safest route home based on public crime data. The application gives each route a 'safety score,' and generates a heat map with interactive and informative markers.

My inspiration for this project came from friends and family who wanted to feel safe when running at night, or when walking in neighborhoods they didn't know well.

Albus: AnatomyX

Medivis: AnatomyX
June 2018 - September 2018

Albus is the database I built and designed to store anatomy part data at Medivis. I created a corresponding REST API and an online data-editing platform for internal and external use.
