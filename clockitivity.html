Clockitivity

Clockitivity is a project I made back in my sophomore year of highschool, and is one of the main reasons I got into computer science. Back in school, I would decide when to do each task straight down to the exact hour and minute, and I wanted to automate this process. I wanted to make an app that would make my life easier, and with this, Clockitivity was born.

Realizing that I could make a computer do my work for me, while still being able to provide my own creative input, showed me the endless possibilities of what I could do. That creative freedom mixed with logical processes and steps made me fall in love with computer science, and to this day, creating productivy tools or automating annoying processes is one of my favorite things to do.
